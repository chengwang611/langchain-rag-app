**Capital Market Risk Review Pipeline — XXX Capital Markets** | Python, LangGraph, LangChain, OpenAI GPT-4o-mini

- Architected a **two-pipeline multi-agent LangGraph system** for XXX Capital Markets supporting many funds with risk report documents ingested daily or hourly: (1) a **batch ingestion pipeline** (`build_ingestion_graph`) that splits, tags, embeds and persists fund documents; (2) an **on-demand review pipeline** (`build_review_graph`) that retrieves by `fund_id`, runs three AI agents, and pauses for HITL approval — decoupling embedding cost from retrieval and ensuring each fund's documents are fully isolated
- Implemented **fund-scoped document ingestion** (`ingest_node`, `embed_and_persist_node`) that tags every chunk with `fund_id` + `report_date` metadata and incrementally appends to a persistent vector store (demo: module-level dict with documented PGVector swap point), supporting multiple hourly or daily ingestion runs per fund without re-embedding historical documents
- Implemented **fund-filtered semantic retrieval** (`retrieve_node`) that loads only the queried `fund_id`'s persisted chunks — Fund A's documents never pollute Fund B's retrieval — with documented upgrade path to `PGVector.similarity_search(query, k=8, filter={"fund_id": fund_id})`
- Implemented a **Regulatory Compliance Agent** (`compliance_agent.py`) with three LangChain tools (`check_basel_threshold`, `get_xxx_risk_appetite`, `generate_remediation_recommendation`) that cross-reference extracted risk findings against Basel III/IV thresholds (BCBS 352 / 325 / 238, SR 11-7) and XXX internal risk appetite limits, auto-generating remediation actions with owner, SLA, and regulatory reference
- Built a **Market Sensitivity Analysis Agent** (`market_agent.py`) with three quantitative tools (`calculate_var_delta`, `estimate_cva_exposure`, `calculate_rwa_impact`) that enrich each finding with 99%/10-day VaR + Basel III IMA capital charges, CVA via EPE × PD × LGD (BCBS 325), and RWA under the Standardised Approach (BCBS 424 / CRR2)
- Implemented a **Risk Escalation Agent** (`escalation_agent.py`) with four notification tools (`classify_findings_by_severity`, `send_slack_notification`, `send_email_notification`, `create_servicenow_ticket`) that classify findings by severity and autonomously route critical findings to senior risk officers via Slack (`#xxx-cm-critical-risk-alerts`), email to designated officers, and ServiceNow P1/P2 incident tickets
- Implemented a **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) interrupt pattern** (`review.py`) using LangGraph checkpointing (`thread_id`), enabling XXX risk analysts to approve, edit, or reject AI-generated findings — enriched with compliance breach flags, VaR/CVA/RWA metrics, and escalation log — before finalization
- Designed a typed **shared state contract** (`ReviewState` TypedDict, 17 fields in `models.py`) covering both pipelines, enforcing clean interfaces between ingestion, retrieval, LLM analysis, agent enrichment, HITL, and finalization stages
